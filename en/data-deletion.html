Request Data Deletion - Raffy

If you have used the Raffy app and want your personal data to be permanently deleted from our systems, you can submit a request by contacting us.

Important Notes

  • If you used Raffy without creating an account, your data is stored only on your device. We do not have access to it and cannot delete it remotely.
  • If you logged in using your Gmail account, some data may be stored securely in our cloud database (via Supabase), and we can delete it upon request.

How to request deletion

Send an email to: gurrulabs@gmail.com

Subject: Data Deletion Request - Raffy

Please include:

  • The email address used to log in (if applicable).
  • Any additional information that helps us locate your data.

We will process your request within 7 days and confirm once your data has been deleted.

If you have any questions, contact us at gurrulabs@gmail.com.
