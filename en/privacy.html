Privacy Policy for Raffy

Welcome to Raffy, a mobile application developed by GurruLabs. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you use our app.

If you have any questions, please contact us at gurrulabs@gmail.com.

1. Overview

Raffy is a parenting and caregiving app designed to help track a child’s growth, sleep, feeding, and doctor visits. This Privacy Policy applies to all users of the Raffy app globally.

2. Data We Collect

We collect the following information, either directly from users or through usage:

2.1 User Data

  • Email address (if account is created via Supabase/Gmail).
  • User authentication tokens (if logged in).

2.2 Child Data

  • Name or alias.
  • Date of birth.
  • Gender.
  • Biometrical information (for example, height and weight).
  • Sleep, feeding, and medical logs.

2.3 Device and Usage Data

  • Device information (OS version and device type).
  • In-app events (screen views, button clicks, feature usage).

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data for the following purposes:

  • To provide core app functionality (tracking growth, sleep, and related records).
  • To sync your data across devices (if logged in).
  • To improve app performance, features, and user experience.
  • To debug technical issues and prevent crashes.
  • To display relevant ads (via Google AdMob).
  • For internal analytics (MixPanel, Google Analytics, Firebase).

4. Optional Account and Sync

  • You can use Raffy without an account (data is stored on your device).
  • If you choose to log in via Gmail (Supabase), your data will be stored securely on Supabase servers.
  • We allow you to sync your information across devices using this account.

5. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services for analytics and ads:

Service Purpose Type of Data Shared
Google Analytics Usage analytics App behavior and interactions
MixPanel Event tracking Screen views and feature usage
Firebase Crash reporting and analytics App performance data
Google AdMob Ads Anonymous device identifiers

These services may independently collect data per their own privacy policies.

6. Children’s Privacy

Raffy does not target children under 13 years of age. However, the app allows parents and caregivers to log information about their children.

We treat this data with heightened security and never use children’s data for advertising or profiling.

7. Data Storage and Security

  • Data is encrypted both in transit and at rest.
  • On-device data is sandboxed and only accessible to the app.
  • Cloud-stored data (if syncing enabled) is stored securely via Supabase (location may vary depending on Supabase data center policy).
  • Users can delete or export their data at any time.

8. Data Deletion and User Control

You have full control over your data:

  • Delete your account and all associated data.
  • Export your data to another platform.
  • Opt out of analytics tracking by adjusting your device settings or contacting us at gurrulabs@gmail.com.

Official data deletion request: Request Data Deletion - Raffy.

9. Consent Mechanism

Upon first use of the Raffy app, users are presented with a consent screen that explains:

  • What data is collected (for example, usage analytics, crash reports, and advertising identifiers).
  • Why the data is collected (for example, to improve the app and show relevant ads).
  • Which third-party services receive the data.

Users are given the ability to:

  • Accept or decline analytics and ad tracking separately.
  • Use the app even if they decline optional tracking features.
  • Review or update consent preferences at any time via the app settings.

Analytics and ads are not initialized until the user gives explicit consent, in compliance with GDPR and CPRA requirements.

10. International Compliance

Raffy is committed to complying with global privacy standards, including:

  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) for users in the European Economic Area.
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and CPRA for California users.

Legal Basis for Processing:

  • Consent (when you voluntarily provide information).
  • Legitimate interest (for example, to improve our services).
  • Contract (when providing core app functionality).

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted in-app and/or via app store listings. Please review it periodically.

12. Contact Us

For questions, requests, or complaints regarding your privacy, contact:

GurruLabs
Email: gurrulabs@gmail.com
