# Raffy SEO Content Calendar (Rolling 6 Months)

## Monthly target
- 4 new EN articles.
- Simultaneous ES/FR/PT localized versions.

## Monthly cluster rotation
1. Week 1: Baby sleep by age (routine, regressions, naps).
2. Week 2: Feeding and solids (BLW, purees, schedules, allergies).
3. Week 3: Growth and percentiles (interpretation, tracking, warning signs).
4. Week 4: Newborn care and vaccines (routine, preparation, checklists).

## Article template checklist
- Primary keyword in title, H1, intro, and meta description.
- 2-4 internal links to related articles in same locale.
- FAQ section when useful for search intent.
- Medical safety disclaimer and pediatric escalation guidance.
- JSON-LD Article with `datePublished` and `dateModified`.

## Publishing checklist
- Route added to `seo/articles.manifest.json`.
- Canonical and hreflang regenerated with `seo:sync`.
- Sitemap regenerated with `seo:sitemap`.
- SEO checks pass with `seo:validate`.
